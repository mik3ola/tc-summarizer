## Privacy Practices

### Single Purpose

**Yes** - The extension has a single purpose: Summarize legal documents when users hover over links.

### Host Permissions

**Yes** - We request access to all URLs because:
- Legal documents appear on websites across the entire internet
- We cannot predict which domains users will visit
- The extension only processes content when users explicitly hover over legal links

See `PERMISSIONS_JUSTIFICATION.md` for detailed explanation.

### User Data

**Yes** - We collect:
- Account information (email, subscription status) - for account management
- Usage statistics (summary count) - for quota enforcement
- Text content (temporary) - sent to backend for summarization, never stored

**No** - We do NOT collect:
- Browsing history
- Personal information beyond account email
- Content of summaries (only count)

See `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` for full details.
